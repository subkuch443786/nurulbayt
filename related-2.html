10 Duas Every Child Should Know

By NurulBayt · 6 min read

Teaching your child to speak to Allah through dua is one of the greatest gifts you can give them. Dua connects them to Allah and builds a lifelong relationship of trust and love.

Here are 10 essential duas that every child should memorize before age 7.

1. Dua Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ
"In the name of Allah."

2. Dua After Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
"All praise is due to Allah."

3. Dua Before Sleeping

بِاسْمِكَ اللَّهُمَّ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا
"In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live."

4. Dua When Entering Bathroom

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ
"O Allah, I seek refuge in You from male and female devils."

5. Dua When Leaving Bathroom

غُفْرَانَكَ
"I ask You for forgiveness."

6. Dua When Sneezing

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
"All praise is due to Allah."

7. Dua When Angry or Sad

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ
"I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Shaytan."

8. Dua for Parents

رَبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا
"My Lord, have mercy on them as they raised me when I was small."

9. Dua for Protection

أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
"I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created."

10. Dua for Good in Dunya & Akhirah

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ
"Our Lord, grant us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the torment of the Fire."

💡 Tip: Start with one dua at a time. Practice daily and reward your child for memorizing.

Final Thoughts

Teaching your child these duas is a beautiful investment in their spiritual growth. May they become a means of protection and blessing, in sha Allah.
